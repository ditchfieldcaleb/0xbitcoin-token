Mineable Gems on Ethereum

Mineable Gem Tokens: The First-Ever Mineable ERC-721 Collectible Token

With mineable gem tokens, you can:
- Mine at whichever difficulty you like!
- Mine at an easier difficulty for more gems
- Mine at a harder difficulty for more valuable gems
- Place your gems on the marketplace to sell them to others
- Buy gems you like on the marketplace


To get started with mining:
- Download any 0xBitcoin-compatible mining software (it must support *solo* mining)
- Follow instructions to set it up
- Contract Address: 0x6478f69cc8da40031d7aaee77bdfd50015e75237 (ropsten only!)
- With the same account, set your target rarity (on the my gems page)
- (Lower rarity = harder to mine)
- (A rarity of 50,000 takes about 2 GTX 1070's 10 minutes to mine)
- (Rarity is directly proportional to hashpower)
